About US

SPONGE GRAPHICS DESIGN SERVICES (formerly Basic Concept Design Studio)

We are a team of professional artists who provide a full spectrum of graphic, web design and print media services.
Our doors opened in 2004 and started with small scaled projects as freelance graphic artists. Today, our team provides solutions for small to medium-scale projects, projects that require creative, innovative solution and high-quality implementation.

We do not have a specific style, we adapt to each and every Clients’ need, We adapt to new trends of the national and international market and do not forget our basics, we are a dynamic team of artist and designers.
If we have a broad world of ideas, we also know how to narrow them down for you. We know that your company image, ad and collateral must offer more than just a pretty picture. We know that your image has to work for you, with graphics tailored to tell your story to potential customers that in the end have a desirable effect on your end.
